What are Number Plates?

Number plates, also known as vehicle registration plates, are unique identifiers assigned to vehicles. They serve as proof that a vehicle is registered with the local authorities and comply with road regulations.

A Brief History

The first number plates were introduced in France in 1893, following the passage of the Paris Police Ordinance. The UK followed in 1903 with the Motor Car Act, mandating all vehicles to display a registration plate. Over the decades, number plates have evolved into collectible items, with some fetching astronomical prices at auctions!
